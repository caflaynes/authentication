The Kingdom of Everwood

Nestled between the towering peaks of the Mistveil Mountains and the lush expanse of the Whispering Woods lies the Kingdom of Everwood. This land is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes and the harmonious coexistence of its diverse inhabitants. From the bustling markets of Stonehelm to the serene shores of Lake Seraphim, Everwood is a realm where adventure awaits at every turn.
